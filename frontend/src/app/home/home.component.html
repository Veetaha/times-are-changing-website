Times are Changing

Times are Changing (Pony Game) - is a game project with different elements of different genres, out of which "CyberPunk" and "Sci-FI" prevail. The game is developed by a group of enthusiasts who take up their particular position in the project.

The action takes place in the future of the pony world. The main character is a mare, she lives in a fairly progressive city, but one day she comes across a document that triggers her interest to explore something new. What exactly will she end up with her adventures and what is going to happen afterwards is up to the player.

The universe of Times are Changing is an alternative world where technology coexists with magic. The main character had been living a quiet life until she occasionally encountered a secret document on the old computer equipment of the company she works for. The government has covered all the evidence of its terrible mistake made in the past, except, as it turns out, one. All the ponies who have already tried to get to the bottom of the truth have gone missing with no apparent trace, and now the sinistrous information is in the hooves of the main character.

What should she do? Should she start her own investigation, endangering her life and the lives of her closest ponies, or leave everything as is and forget about it, like a bad dream? Each of the chosen options has many ramifications, where the player will face a moral choice, since even the slightest detail matters.

In addition to the main story, there are side-quests, that are always available for you. Throughout the journey, the player will be accompanied by a deep atmosphere of the surrounding world, consisting of beautiful landscapes and ambient, an excellent ad-hoc soundtrack, as well as music from other popular composers.

You can play from the first person and from the third, where the second option implies no graphic interface and holograms, which increases the realism of the gameplay. For the future we've planned to add a multiplayer, that will include tons of game modes.

The game is still a work in progress at the moment ...
